Kaguya-sama: Love Is War is a romantic comedy anime/manga series that follows the story of two high school students, Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, who are secretly in love with each other but are too proud to confess their feelings. Ohsama Sentai King-Ohger is a Japanese tokusatsu television series that aired in March 5, 2023. It follows a team of superheroes, known as the King-Ohgers, who use their special powers and mecha to protect Earth from evil invaders.
