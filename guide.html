Guideline Rules

This will display the most common questions for the website and give a brief rundown on how the website runs, what are the community guidelines, and more ways you can contact the staff as well as our PO Box and business/email address.

1. Be respectful

Please treat other users with respect and kindness. We do not tolerate hate speech, discrimination, or bullying of any kind.

2. No spam

Do not use the website to post spam or irrelevant content. You will be reported.

3.No A.I. Generated Art

A.I. generated art is strictly prohibited and will be monitored if reported.

4.No illegal or harmful content

Posting or sharing any illegal, harmful, or offensive content is strictly prohibited.

5. Follow community guidelines

This is pretty self-explanatory. Make sure to follow the specific community guidelines and rules set by this page to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all users.

6. Contact Information

Email: user@wip.org | Phone:201-268-8442

7. Report inappropriate content

If you come across any content that violates the guidelines or is inappropriate, please report it to the website administrators immediately by using the contact page.

8. Use the website responsibly

Use the website responsibly and in accordance with its intended purpose. Do not engage in any activities that may harm the website or its users.
